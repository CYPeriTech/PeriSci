## 说明 | Notes

- AUTHORS 文件用于记录**事实上的贡献归属**，不等同于维护权限或决策权。  
- 项目的维护与治理结构请参见 `MAINTAINERS.md`。  
- 学术引用请参见 `CITATION.cff`。

- AUTHORS 文件中的作者列表不自动等同于学术引用作者列表，学术引用请始终以 `CITATION.cff` 为准。

- The AUTHORS file records **factual contribution attribution** and does not imply
  maintenance authority or decision-making power.  
- Project governance and maintenance responsibilities are defined in `MAINTAINERS.md`.  
- For academic citation, refer to `CITATION.cff`.
- The list of authors in AUTHORS.md does not automatically define
  the list of authors for academic citation. Please always refer to `CITATION.cff` for citation purposes.
